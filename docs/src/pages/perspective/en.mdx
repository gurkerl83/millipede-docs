# Necessity of changing perspectives

A multifaceted set of motivations leads to the preparation of this paper.

Among other things the paper serves as a position paper to allow an overview or
orientation of this problem, which is considered to be extremely complex. For
this purpose, the problem is broken down into individual partial problems that
are easy to understand.

The aim is to sensitise the awareness of decision-makers to focus on specific
topics that are considered to be the cause of the problem.
